Common Concerns

You Might Be Thinking One of These

Honest answers to the questions people hesitate to ask.

?I'm worried I won't qualify

Most people are surprised by what they can actually borrow. Even with imperfect credit, a low deposit, or complex income, there are lenders built for your situation. The only way to find out is to look — that's what the free consultation is for.

?I don't want to be pressured into anything

James's entire business runs on referrals. That only works when people trust him. You will never be pushed, upsold, or rushed. If the timing isn't right for you, he'll be the first to say so.

?Can't I just go direct to my bank?

You can — but your bank only shows you their own products. James searches hundreds across the whole market. In most cases he finds a better rate, and his advice costs you nothing.

?I'm self-employed — isn't it too complicated?

Self-employed clients make up a large part of James's caseload. He knows exactly which lenders assess income correctly for sole traders and directors. Don't write yourself off before speaking to someone who handles this every day.

?What's the catch with 'free'?

For most residential cases, James earns a fee from the lender when your mortgage completes — just like any broker. There's no hidden cost to you. Any fee that applies is always agreed in writing before a single piece of work begins.

?Is now even a good time to buy?

That depends on your situation — not the news. James will give you an honest view of where you stand right now, so you're making a decision based on facts, not headlines.
